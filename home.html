Vignan's Nirula Institute Of Technology And Science For Women

Popular degrees offered at Vignan's Nirula Institute of Technology and Science for Women include B.Tech, MBA, M.Tech. Focus is given to activities beyond academics at Vignan's Nirula Institute of Technology and Science for Women, which is evident from its infrastructure, extracurricular activities and national & international collaborations.
